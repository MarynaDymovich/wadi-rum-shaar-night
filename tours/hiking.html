Hike 1

Jebel Umm Al-Dami: Summit of the Stars Expedition

Jebel Umm Al-Dami, the highest peak in Wadi Rum, has long been a symbol of majesty and endurance. Its name translates to “Mother of the Blood” in Arabic, referencing local legends and its imposing presence in the desert. Historically, it has served as a landmark for nomadic Bedouins and traders traversing the desert, guiding them through the vast expanse of Wadi Rum.

Our half-day adventure begins from Wadi Rum village, where you’ll meet your guide and set out towards the base of Jebel Umm Al-Dami. As we drive through the desert, the grandeur of the peak becomes evident.

The hike takes you through diverse terrain, from sandy stretches to rocky paths, offering a true sense of the desert’s rugged beauty. As you ascend, your guide will share stories of the peak’s historical significance and its role in the lives of ancient desert dwellers.

Upon reaching the summit, you’ll be greeted by breathtaking panoramic views of the desert stretching to the horizon. The clear sky and expansive views perfectly embody the “Summit of the Stars,” making this climb a memorable blend of adventure and natural wonder.

After soaking in the stunning vistas, we’ll return to Wadi Rum village, where you’ll have gained a deeper appreciation for the desert’s heritage and beauty in a compact yet impactful journey.

Duration: Half Day

Price: 1 Person 70 JOD

Hike 2

Jebel Al-Hish: Desert Mirage Trail Challenge

Jebel Al-Hish stands as a sentinel in the Wadi Rum desert, a prominent peak known for its rugged grandeur and rich historical significance. This striking mountain is part of the larger Jebel Rum massif and has been a landmark in the region for centuries. The name "Al-Hish" translates to "the ridge" in Arabic, reflecting its prominent position in the landscape.

Historically, Jebel Al-Hish has been a waypoint for desert travelers and Bedouins, offering a crucial reference point amidst the vast desert. Its towering presence has been a part of local folklore and a beacon for those navigating the ancient trade routes that once crisscrossed this desert expanse.

Begin your half-day adventure from Wadi Rum village with our experienced guides. After a short drive, you'll arrive at the base of Jebel Al-Hish, ready to tackle the trail.

The hike features a blend of sandy stretches and rocky paths, showcasing Wadi Rum’s diverse landscape. As you ascend, your guide will share fascinating stories about the mountain’s historical significance and its role in ancient desert trade.

Reaching the summit, you'll be rewarded with sweeping views that make the desert appear like a mirage. This hike is a perfect blend of history, challenge, and stunning scenery, providing a deep connection with the ancient desert and its majestic beauty.

Duration: Half Day

Price: 1 Person 70 JOD

Hike 3

Jebel Barada: Ancient Echoes Path Journey

Jebel Barada, known for its distinctive rock formations and ancient significance, stands proudly in the heart of Wadi Rum. The name "Barada" translates to "the waterfall" in Arabic, a nod to the area’s historical water sources that once sustained the desert's inhabitants. This peak has served as a pivotal landmark throughout history, guiding caravans and nomads across the arid landscape.

Start your half-day adventure from Wadi Rum village, where you’ll meet your guide and prepare for an exciting trek. We’ll drive you to the base of Jebel Barada, where the journey begins.

The trail winds through a fascinating mix of sandy paths and rugged rock formations, reflecting the diverse beauty of the desert. As you hike, your guide will share insights into the historical role of Jebel Barada, including its significance as a waypoint for ancient trade routes and its role in local legends.

Reaching the summit, you'll be rewarded with panoramic views that reveal the vastness and intricate patterns of the desert. The serene landscape, combined with the echoes of history, makes this hike a profound experience.

After taking in the spectacular scenery, we’ll return to Wadi Rum village, concluding a journey that offers a deep connection with the ancient echoes and timeless beauty of Wadi Rum.

Duration: Half Day

Price: 1 Person 70 JOD
