Tour 1

Desert Dash: 2-Hour Jeep Safari

Short on time? This 2-hour jeep safari is perfect for a quick yet unforgettable adventure in Wadi Rum. We’ll whisk you away to iconic sites such as Lawrence’s Spring, the majestic Khazali Canyon, the stunning Red Sand Dune, and the Little Rock Bridge. Experience the thrill of climbing towering dunes and capturing breathtaking photos of the desert landscape. Of course, no visit to Wadi Rum is complete without a refreshing cup of traditional Bedouin tea, served with the warm hospitality that defines the desert experience.

  • 2-3 Person 30 JOD
  • 4+ Person 25 JOD

  • Ain Abu Aina (Lawrence’s Spring)
  • Wadi Khazali (Khazali Canyon)
  • The Red Sand Dune
  • The Little Rock Bridge

Included:

  • Bedouin Tea
  • Water

Tour 2

Wadi Wonders: Half-Day Jeep Adventure

Immerse yourself in the wonders of Wadi Rum on this half-day jeep adventure. Over four hours, we'll explore some of the desert's most awe-inspiring locations, including the ancient Anfishiyeh Inscriptions, the towering Umm Fruth Rock Bridge, and the serene Wadi Abu Khashaba. You'll also visit the famed Lawrence’s Spring and Khazali Canyon. Between stops, we’ll pause to enjoy a cup of Bedouin tea by a crackling fire, a perfect way to soak in the tranquil desert atmosphere. This tour is ideal for those looking to experience the best of Wadi Rum in a morning or afternoon.

  • 4-5 Person 35 JOD

Tour 3

Red Sands Odyssey: Full-Day Jeep Exploration

Dive deep into the heart of Wadi Rum with this full-day jeep exploration. Starting at the base of Burdah Rock Bridge, the largest natural arch in the region, you'll journey through the desert's wild, untamed beauty. Explore the striking Khashaba Canyon with its unique red and white sandstone layers, climb monumental dunes, and traverse iconic rock bridges. Along the way, we'll pause for a traditional Bedouin lunch in a picturesque setting, complemented by breaks for tea in the shade of towering cliffs. This is a comprehensive adventure that showcases the very best of Wadi Rum’s dramatic landscapes.

Price:

  • 1 Person 55 JOD
  • 2-3 Person 50 JOD
  • 4-5 Person 45 JOD

You Visit:

  • Khor Al Ajram Valley
  • Ain Abu Aina (Lawrence’s Spring)
  • Wadi Khazali (Khazali Canyon)
  • The Red Sand Dune
  • The Little Rock Bridge
  • Anfishiyeh Inscriptions
  • Umm Fruth Rock Bridge
  • Geber Amra Canyon
  • Burdah Rock Bridge, the largest natural arch in Wadi Rum
  • Khashaba Canyon

Included:

  • Bedouin Tea
  • Lunch
  • Water
