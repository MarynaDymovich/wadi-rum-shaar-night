Tour 2

Wadi Wonders: Half-Day Jeep Adventure

Join me for a four-hour jeep tour to explore Wadi Rum like never before. We'll visit amazing places such as Lawrence’s Spring, Khazali Canyon, and the beautiful sand dunes. You’ll also see the ancient Anfishiyeh inscriptions, Small Arch, and Umm Fruth Rock Bridge, plus a hike through Wadi Abu Khashaba. We’ll take a break for some desert tea and build a fire to relax. You'll have the chance to explore each site on foot, climbing dunes, walking over rock bridges and, of course, taking beautiful photos. It’s the perfect way to truly experience the desert if you visit just in the morning or afternoon.

Tour 3

Red Sands Odyssey: Full-Day Jeep Exploration

This full-day jeep adventure will take you deeper into the mesmerizing Wadi Rum desert, where you'll get to explore even more stunning landscapes. We'll start by visiting the base of Burdah Rock Bridge, the largest natural arch in the area. As we venture further, you'll experience the raw beauty of the desert, with its wild scenery and dramatic rock formations. We’ll take short breaks for some desert tea and a longer break to have Bedouin lunch and relax Bedouin style. You'll have the opportunity to hike through the scenic Khashaba Canyon, known for its striking red and white hues. Along the way, you'll climb towering dunes and explore other fascinating sites by foot. Whether you're scaling rock bridges or enjoying the serene desert vistas, you will spend a memorable day discovering the magic of Wadi Rum’s natural wonders.
