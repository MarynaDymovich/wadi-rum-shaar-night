Tour 1

Half Day Jeep Tour and Night in the Desert

Explore Wadi Rum with a half-day jeep tour followed by a night in the desert. It's a perfect program designed for those who arrives in Wadi Rum in the afternoon or later at night. We will do a four-hour jeep tour to explore Wadi Rum in the afternoon when you arrive or in the morning next day. We'll visit amazing places such as Lawrence’s Spring, Khazali Canyon, and the beautiful sand dunes. You’ll also see the ancient Anfishiyeh inscriptions, Small Arch, and Umm Fruth Rock Bridge, plus hike through Wadi Abu Khashaba. We’ll take a break for some desert tea and build a fire to relax. You'll have the chance to explore each site on foot, climbing dunes, walking over rock bridges and, of course, taking beautiful photos. Wrap the day by watching the sunset from a scenic spot. Then enjoy Bedouin dinner and a comfortable night in the desert camp or camp under the stars in the desert, followed by a good breakfast next morning.

  • Anfishiyeh Inscriptions
  • Umm Fruth Rock Bridge
  • Geber Amra Canyon
  • Abu Khashaba Canyon
  • Sunset

  • Includes dinner, breakfast, and water

Tour 2

Day Jeep Tour and Night in the Desert

If you have a full day and a night in Wadi Rum, this tour maximizes your time in the desert with both adventure and relaxation. We start the day with a jeep tour covering highlights such as Lawrence’s Spring, Red Sand Dune, Khazali Canyon, Little Arch, Abu Khashaba Canyon, Umm Frouth Arch, Burdah Rock Bridge, Mushroom Rock, and Burrah Canyon. We’ll take short breaks for some desert tea and a longer break to have Bedouin lunch and relax Bedouin style. You'll have the opportunity to hike through the scenic Khashaba Canyon, known for its striking red and white hues. For a quieter experience, you can choose to explore Wadi Rum’s South Desert, a more remote and serene area perfect for hiking. Wrap the day by watching the sunset from a scenic spot. Then enjoy Bedouin dinner and a comfortable night in the desert camp or camping under the stars in the desert, followed by a good breakfast next morning.

Tour 4

Jeep Tour, Hiking, and Night in the Desert

This two day adventure will take you deeper into the mesmerizing Wadi Rum desert, where you'll get to explore even more stunning landscapes. On the first day we will do a classical full day jeep tour with Bedouin lunch, stops for tea and relaxation. As we venture into the desert, you'll experience the raw beauty of the Wadi RUm, with its wild scenery and dramatic rock formations. After a day of exploration, you’ll enjoy a tranquil evening camping in the desert, with a view of the star-filled sky, or in a comfortable traditional Bedouin camp. The next day, you'll hike up the impressive Jebel Al Hash for amazing views of the desert. Then, you'll visit the famous Burdah Rock Bridge, a beautiful natural arch. This hike provides stunning scenery and great photo spots, making for a memorable day in Wadi Rum.
