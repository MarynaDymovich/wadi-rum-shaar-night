WADI RUM

SHA'AR NIGHT

Step into the enchanting Wadi Rum desert with a half-day jeep tour, perfect for those arriving in the late afternoon or evening. As you begin your journey, the desert's golden hues begin to shift as the sun starts to set, casting a magical glow over the rugged landscape. Your adventure kicks off with a visit to Khor Al Ajram Valley, where you'll traverse through wide-open expanses surrounded by towering sandstone cliffs. The jeep takes you to Lawrence’s Spring a historic site with natural water sources where you can enjoy a serene pause. From here, the route leads to Wadi Khazali (Khazali Canyon), a narrow gorge adorned with ancient inscriptions and petroglyphs that tell stories of the desert's past inhabitants.

The journey continues to the Red Sand Dune, where you can climb to the top for a panoramic view of the surrounding desert. The route then brings you to the Little Rock Bridge, a natural sandstone arch, and Anfishiyeh Inscriptions, where centuries-old carvings in the rock are a testament to the region’s rich history. Next, you'll traverse the Umm Fruth Rock Bridge, a larger and more iconic arch that offers an exhilarating opportunity for a short climb.

As the day draws to a close, you'll witness the sunset over the dunes, painting the desert in a palette of reds and purples. After a day of exploration, unwind with a warm Bedouin tea by a crackling fire, followed by a hearty Bedouin dinner. You’ll spend the night under a canopy of stars or in a cozy desert camp, lulled to sleep by the tranquil sounds of the desert. Wake up to a peaceful morning and enjoy a delicious breakfast surrounded by the serene beauty of the desert, completing a truly unforgettable experience

Embark on an epic two-day adventure through Wadi Rum, where the thrill of off-road exploration meets the serenity of a full-day hike and a night under the stars. This journey is crafted for those who seek a deeper connection with the desert, offering a perfect blend of adventure and tranquility.

Day 1: Jeep Tour & Overnight Camp Your adventure begins with a full-day jeep tour through the most iconic and remote areas of Wadi Rum. The journey starts with a drive through Khor Al Ajram Valley, a dramatic landscape of towering sandstone cliffs and expansive sand plains. Here, you’ll experience the true scale of the desert as you traverse its rugged terrain. The first stop is Lawrence’s Spring, a historic site where you’ll delve into the legacy of the legendary Lawrence of Arabia and the Bedouin tribes who have thrived in this harsh environment for centuries.

From here, continue to Wadi Khazali (Khazali Canyon), a narrow passageway rich with ancient petroglyphs and inscriptions. As you walk through the canyon, you’ll encounter carvings that depict scenes from everyday life, offering a glimpse into the desert’s deep history. The tour then takes you to the Red Sand Dune, where you can climb to the top for a panoramic view of the surrounding desert, a perfect spot for stunning photos.

Next, you’ll visit the Little Rock Bridge and the Umm Fruth Rock Bridge, two awe-inspiring natural arches sculpted by centuries of wind and erosion. These sites provide unique photo opportunities and a chance to marvel at nature's artistry. The jeep tour also includes stops at remote areas, where you’ll experience the solitude and vastness of the desert while savoring a traditional Bedouin lunch amidst breathtaking scenery.

As the day winds down, head to your desert camp, where you’ll be welcomed with warm Bedouin hospitality. Unwind with a cup of sweet Bedouin tea and enjoy a traditional dinner around a crackling campfire. As night falls, the desert sky comes alive with countless stars, offering a mesmerizing celestial display. You can choose to sleep in a cozy Bedouin tent or under the open sky, enveloped by the tranquil beauty of the desert.

Day 2: Full-Day Hike & Departure The second day begins with an early breakfast to fuel your adventure. The hike starts with a challenging trek to Jebel Al Hash, where a climb rewards you with stunning panoramic views of the desert below. Traverse through varied terrain, from sandy plains to rocky outcrops, showcasing the diverse landscapes of Wadi Rum.

After descending from Jebel Al Hash, continue your hike through the lesser-known areas of Wadi Rum, away from typical tourist paths. Pass through Khashaba Canyon, a serene and secluded spot where the silence of the desert is almost palpable. Enjoy a packed lunch amidst the stunning landscape, with ample time to absorb the sights and sounds of the desert.

The hike concludes in the late afternoon as you return to your camp or pick-up point, leaving you with unforgettable memories of Wadi Rum’s epic landscapes and the profound peace that comes from exploring its remote corners.
