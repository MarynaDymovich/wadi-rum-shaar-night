Tour 1

Twilight Trails: Half-Day Jeep & Overnight

Step into the enchanting Wadi Rum desert with a half-day jeep tour, perfect for those arriving in the late afternoon or evening. As you begin your journey, the desert's golden hues begin to shift as the sun starts to set, casting a magical glow over the rugged landscape. Your adventure kicks off with a visit to Khor Al Ajram Valley, where you'll traverse through wide-open expanses surrounded by towering sandstone cliffs. The jeep takes you to Lawrence’s Spring a historic site with natural water sources where you can enjoy a serene pause. From here, the route leads to Wadi Khazali (Khazali Canyon), a narrow gorge adorned with ancient inscriptions and petroglyphs that tell stories of the desert's past inhabitants.

The journey continues to the Red Sand Dune, where you can climb to the top for a panoramic view of the surrounding desert. The route then brings you to the Little Rock Bridge, a natural sandstone arch, and Anfishiyeh Inscriptions, where centuries-old carvings in the rock are a testament to the region’s rich history. Next, you'll traverse the Umm Fruth Rock Bridge, a larger and more iconic arch that offers an exhilarating opportunity for a short climb.

As the day draws to a close, you'll witness the sunset over the dunes, painting the desert in a palette of reds and purples. After a day of exploration, unwind with a warm Bedouin tea by a crackling fire, followed by a hearty Bedouin dinner. You’ll spend the night under a canopy of stars or in a cozy desert camp, lulled to sleep by the tranquil sounds of the desert. Wake up to a peaceful morning and enjoy a delicious breakfast surrounded by the serene beauty of the desert, completing a truly unforgettable experience

Highlights:

  • Scenic jeep tour through iconic Wadi Rum sites
  • Exploration of Lawrence’s Spring, Khazali Canyon, and Red Sand Dunes
  • Bedouin tea, dinner, and breakfast included
  • Overnight stay in the desert under the stars or in a comfortable camp

Tour 2

Sunset to Sunrise: Full-Day Jeep & Camp

Maximize your Wadi Rum adventure with a full-day jeep tour that immerses you in the desert’s highlights. Your journey begins with a drive through Khor Al Ajram Valley, a dramatic and expansive valley that sets the tone for the adventure ahead. The first stop is Lawrence’s Spring, a historical site offering a glimpse into the life of the Bedouins and the legendary Lawrence of Arabia. As you continue, you'll arrive at the Red Sand Dune, where you can climb to the top and marvel at the vastness of the desert below.

Next, the tour takes you to Wadi Khazali (Khazali Canyon), a narrow canyon rich with ancient inscriptions and carvings. This passageway reveals stories from the past, etched into the rock walls. The jeep then brings you to the Little Rock Bridge, where the natural arch formation provides a stunning backdrop for photos. Continuing on, you’ll explore the Abu Khashaba Canyon, a secluded spot where you can take a short hike through the canyon’s narrow passages.

The highlight of the tour is the visit to Burdah Rock Bridge, the largest natural arch in Wadi Rum, where you can admire the vastness of the desert from this impressive vantage point. The route also includes a stop at the surreal Mushroom Rock, a unique sandstone formation shaped by centuries of wind and erosion.

After a day filled with exploration, enjoy a traditional Bedouin lunch and unwind with tea breaks amidst the stunning landscapes. The day ends with a picturesque sunset, where the desert transforms into a canvas of vivid colors. As night falls, you’ll indulge in a delicious Bedouin dinner and spend the night under the stars or in a traditional camp. Wake up to a refreshing breakfast, ready to take on another day of desert beauty

Tour 3

Desert Dreams: Jeep Tour, Hike & Night Under Stars

Embark on an unforgettable journey through the heart of Wadi Rum, where the rugged beauty of the desert is paired with thrilling adventures and tranquil moments. This two-day, two-night experience offers the perfect blend of exploration and relaxation, with jeep tours, scenic hikes, and starlit nights in the desert.

Day 1: Jeep Tour & First Night in the Desert
Your adventure begins with a full-day jeep tour that takes you deep into the mesmerizing landscapes of Wadi Rum. The journey starts at Khor Al Ajram Valley, where towering cliffs and expansive sand plains set the stage for the epic adventure ahead. The first stop is Lawrence’s Spring, a historic site rich in both natural beauty and cultural significance. Here, you’ll learn about the legendary Lawrence of Arabia and the Bedouin tribes who have lived in this harsh but beautiful environment for centuries.

Next, you’ll venture to Wadi Khazali (Khazali Canyon), a narrow gorge adorned with ancient petroglyphs and inscriptions that tell the stories of the desert’s early inhabitants. As you navigate the canyon’s winding passageways, you’ll be captivated by the sense of history and mystery that permeates the air. The tour continues to the iconic Red Sand Dune, where you can climb to the top for breathtaking views of the desert. This is a perfect spot to capture stunning photos and appreciate the vastness of Wadi Rum.

The adventure doesn’t stop there. You’ll visit the natural wonders of Little Rock Bridge and Umm Fruth Rock Bridge, where centuries of wind and erosion have sculpted incredible rock formations. These sites offer unique opportunities for exploration and photography. The jeep tour also includes stops at remote areas, where you can enjoy the solitude and silence of the desert while savoring a traditional Bedouin lunch.

As the sun begins to set, you’ll arrive at your desert camp, where warm Bedouin hospitality awaits. Relax with a cup of sweet tea and enjoy a traditional dinner around a crackling campfire. The evening offers a chance to gaze at the night sky, filled with countless stars that illuminate the desert in a soft, ethereal glow. You can choose to sleep in a cozy Bedouin tent or under the open sky, surrounded by the tranquil beauty of the desert.

Day 2: Scenic Hike & Second Night in the Desert
The second day begins with a hearty breakfast to prepare you for a full day of hiking. The hike starts with a trek to Jebel Al Hash, a challenging climb that offers panoramic views of the desert. As you ascend, you’ll pass through varied terrain, from sandy plains to rocky outcrops, showcasing the diverse landscapes of Wadi Rum. The summit rewards you with a stunning vista that stretches as far as the eye can see, a true highlight of the journey.

After descending from Jebel Al Hash, the hike continues through the serene and secluded Khashaba Canyon, a place of profound peace and beauty. Here, you can enjoy a packed lunch amidst the stunning scenery, with plenty of time to relax and take in the sights and sounds of the desert. The hike offers a deeper connection to the natural world, away from the usual tourist paths, allowing you to experience Wadi Rum in its purest form.

As the day draws to a close, you’ll return to your camp for a second night under the stars. Enjoy a delicious dinner and another opportunity to stargaze in the clear desert sky, reflecting on the day’s adventures.

Day 3: Morning Departure
On the third day, wake up to the peaceful sounds of the desert and enjoy a final breakfast at your camp. After packing up, you’ll have time for a short walk or simply relax before heading back to your starting point, leaving Wadi Rum with unforgettable memories of your desert duo adventure.

Highlights:

  • Full-day jeep tour covering iconic and remote Wadi Rum sites
  • Two nights in a Bedouin camp with dinner and stargazing
  • Full-day hike to Jebel Al Hash and through Khashaba Canyon
  • Bedouin tea, lunch, dinner, and breakfast included
  • Stunning views, ancient petroglyphs, and natural rock formations

Embark on an epic two-day adventure through Wadi Rum, where the thrill of off-road exploration meets the serenity of a full-day hike and a night under the stars. This journey is crafted for those who seek a deeper connection with the desert, offering a perfect blend of adventure and tranquility.

Day 1: Jeep Tour & Overnight Camp Your adventure begins with a full-day jeep tour through the most iconic and remote areas of Wadi Rum. From here, continue to Wadi Khazali (Khazali Canyon), a narrow passageway rich with ancient petroglyphs and inscriptions. The tour then takes you to the Red Sand Dune, where you can climb to the top for a panoramic view of the surrounding desert, a perfect spot for stunning photos.

Next, you’ll visit the Little Rock Bridge and the Umm Fruth Rock Bridge, two awe-inspiring natural arches sculpted by centuries of wind and erosion. These sites provide unique photo opportunities and a chance to marvel at nature's artistry. The jeep tour also includes stops at remote areas, where you’ll experience the solitude and vastness of the desert while savoring a traditional Bedouin lunch amidst breathtaking scenery.

As the day winds down, head to your desert camp, where you’ll be welcomed with warm Bedouin hospitality. Unwind with a cup of sweet Bedouin tea and enjoy a traditional dinner around a crackling campfire. As night falls, the desert sky comes alive with countless stars, offering a mesmerizing celestial display. The hike concludes in the late afternoon as you return to your camp or pick-up point, leaving you with unforgettable memories of Wadi Rum’s epic landscapes and the profound peace that comes from exploring its remote corners.
