Tour 1

Desert Duo: Jeep Tour & Hike with Two Nights

Experience the best of Wadi Rum on this unforgettable adventure that perfectly blends the excitement of off-road exploration with the tranquility of desert hikes. This two-day journey offers a unique opportunity to immerse yourself in the captivating beauty of the desert.

Day 1: Begin your Wadi Rum adventure with a full-day jeep tour designed to showcase the most breathtaking features of this otherworldly desert. Your journey kicks off with a thrilling ride through the expansive Khor Al Ajram Valley, a vast and dramatic landscape that sets the stage for the day's exploration.
The first stop is the iconic Lawrence’s Spring, a historical site offering fascinating insights into the Bedouin way of life and the legendary Lawrence of Arabia. This tranquil spring has been a vital water source in the desert for centuries. Next, ascend the striking Red Sand Dune, where the sweeping desert views from the top are nothing short of mesmerizing. Feel the thrill of climbing and the joy of surveying the endless desert expanse below.

Continue your adventure with a visit to Wadi Khazali (Khazali Canyon), a narrow and captivating canyon rich with ancient inscriptions and rock carvings that reveal the stories of the desert's past inhabitants. Explore the Little Rock Bridge, a natural arch formation providing a perfect spot for photographs and moments of awe. Embark on a short yet invigorating hike through the serene Abu Khashaba Canyon, where you can experience the desert's tranquil beauty up close.

The pinnacle of your day is a visit to the Burdah Rock Bridge, the largest natural arch in Wadi Rum. Admire the desert's vastness from this unparalleled vantage point before concluding your exploration with a visit to the Mushroom Rock, a unique sandstone formation sculpted by the winds over millennia.

After a day full of adventure, indulge in a traditional Bedouin lunch and savor relaxing tea breaks amidst the stunning landscapes. As the sun sets, the desert transforms into a canvas of vivid hues. Enjoy a hearty Bedouin dinner under the stars or in a cozy traditional camp, and experience the tranquil beauty of the desert night.

Day 2: Start your morning with a refreshing breakfast, gearing up for another day of desert discovery. Spend the morning delving deeper into the Wadi Rum landscape, with opportunities for additional scenic hikes or jeep tours tailored to your preferences. The serene beauty of the desert will provide a perfect backdrop for this memorable adventure.

Highlights:

  • Full-day jeep tour through Khor Al Ajram Valley, Lawrence’s Spring, Red Sand Dune, and Wadi Khazali (Khazali Canyon)
  • Visit to Little Rock Bridge and Abu Khashaba Canyon
  • Admire the Burdah Rock Bridge and the Mushroom Rock
  • Traditional Bedouin lunch, tea breaks, dinner, and breakfast included
  • Two nights in the desert with options to sleep under the stars or in cozy traditional Bedouin tents

Tour 2

Starlit Serenity: Two Nights in the Desert

Immerse yourself in the captivating beauty of Wadi Rum with this two-day adventure that offers a deeper connection with the desert. Your journey begins with a full-day jeep tour, taking you through the heart of Wadi Rum’s most iconic locations. The route winds through Khor Al Ajram Valley, a vast and scenic valley that introduces you to the scale and grandeur of the desert. The tour includes visits to key sites such as Lawrence’s Spring, where you can see the natural water source that has sustained Bedouins for centuries, and Khazali Canyon, a narrow gorge with ancient inscriptions that provide a glimpse into the lives of the desert’s past inhabitants.

The journey continues to the Red Sand Dune, where you can climb to the summit and enjoy a sweeping view of the desert landscape. You’ll also visit the Little Rock Bridge and the Umm Fruth Rock Bridge, both of which offer unique natural formations and excellent photo opportunities. The day is punctuated with tea breaks and a traditional Bedouin lunch, allowing you to savor the flavors of the desert.

As the day draws to a close, retreat to your camp where you can choose to sleep under a star-studded sky or in a comfortable Bedouin tent. The second day begins with a hike up Jebel Al Hash, a mountain that offers stunning panoramic views of the desert. Later, visit the majestic Burdah Rock Bridge, capturing unforgettable moments amidst the desert's serene landscape. This tour provides a perfect escape for nature lovers, offering a chance to connect deeply with the natural beauty and tranquility of Wadi Rum.

Highlights:

  • Extensive jeep tour covering all major Wadi Rum sites
  • Hike up Jebel Al Hash and visit to Burdah Rock Bridge
