Tour 1

Desert Duo: Jeep Tour & Hike with Two Nights

**Discover the beauty and magic of Wadi Rum on this unforgettable two-night adventure.** You'll explore the desert's stunning landscapes, experience Bedouin culture, and sleep under a sky full of stars.

Night 1: Your journey begins as you arrive at a traditional Bedouin camp just in time to watch the sun set over the desert. Enjoy a delicious dinner of local dishes, followed by a cozy evening around the campfire. Listen to stories of the desert and relax under the twinkling stars before settling into your tent for the night.

Day 1: After breakfast, get ready for a full-day jeep tour through the most beautiful spots in Wadi Rum. Start with the vast Khor Al Ajram Valley, then visit Lawrence’s Spring, where you’ll learn about the history of Lawrence of Arabia. Next, climb the Red Sand Dune for amazing views of the desert.
Continue your adventure at Wadi Khazali (Khazali Canyon), where ancient carvings tell stories of the past. Visit the Little Rock Bridge and take a peaceful hike through Abu Khashaba Canyon.

The day’s highlight is the impressive Burdah Rock Bridge, the largest arch in Wadi Rum. Before heading back to camp, stop by the unique Mushroom Rock, shaped by the wind over many years.

During the day, you’ll enjoy a traditional Bedouin lunch with refreshing tea breaks. As the day ends, have dinner under the stars and get ready for another magical night in the desert.

Night 2: For your second night, you have a special treat—sleeping outside under the stars. Lay down on soft blankets and let the quiet of the desert surround you as you drift off to sleep beneath a sky full of shimmering stars. It's a night you’ll never forget.

Day 2: Start your morning with a hearty breakfast as the desert awakens around you. Spend the morning exploring more of Wadi Rum with another jeep tour or a hike, depending on what you feel like. This is your chance to soak in the peaceful beauty of the desert one last time before your adventure ends.

Highlights:

  • Full-day jeep tour through Khor Al Ajram Valley, Lawrence’s Spring, Red Sand Dune, and Wadi Khazali (Khazali Canyon)
  • Visit to Little Rock Bridge and Abu Khashaba Canyon
  • Admire the Burdah Rock Bridge and the Mushroom Rock
  • Traditional Bedouin lunch, tea breaks, dinner, and breakfast included
  • Two nights in the desert with options to sleep under the stars or in cozy traditional Bedouin tents

Tour 2

Starlit Serenity: One Day & One Night in the Desert

**Step into the heart of Wadi Rum and let the desert's ancient magic capture your soul.** This one-day, one-night journey is a perfect escape for those who wish to connect deeply with the serene beauty of the desert.

Day 1: Your adventure begins with a full-day jeep tour through the most breathtaking landscapes of Wadi Rum. Start in the wide and peaceful Khor Al Ajram Valley, where the vastness of the desert welcomes you. The tour takes you to Lawrence’s Spring, a vital water source that has refreshed Bedouins for generations. From there, explore the mystical Khazali Canyon, where ancient inscriptions whisper the stories of the past.

The journey continues to the towering Red Sand Dune, where you can climb to the top and marvel at the sweeping views of the desert. Visit the unique Little Rock Bridge and the famous Umm Fruth Rock Bridge, two natural wonders shaped by time and wind, perfect for capturing unforgettable photos. During the day, you’ll enjoy a traditional Bedouin lunch with refreshing tea breaks, giving you a taste of the desert's rich flavors.

Night 1: As the sun sets, return to your camp, where a special night awaits you. Choose to sleep in a cozy Bedouin tent or under the vast, star-filled sky. As the desert quiets down, let the gentle breeze and the sparkling stars lull you to sleep, creating memories you’ll cherish forever.

Day 2: Wake up to the soft light of dawn and a hearty breakfast, ready for a new adventure. Begin the day with a hike up Jebel Al Hash, a mountain that offers panoramic views of the endless desert. Later, visit the majestic Burdah Rock Bridge, one of Wadi Rum’s most famous landmarks. Capture the beauty and tranquility of the desert one last time before your journey comes to an end.

Night 2: After the day's exploration, return to the camp. You will enjoy another peaceful night under the stars or in a comfortable Bedouin tent, soaking in the tranquility of the desert night before concluding your adventure.

Highlights:

  • Full-day jeep tour through Khor Al Ajram Valley, Lawrence’s Spring, Khazali Canyon, and Red Sand Dune
  • Visit to Little Rock Bridge and Umm Fruth Rock Bridge
  • Hike up Jebel Al Hash and visit Burdah Rock Bridge
  • One night in the desert with options to sleep under the stars or in Bedouin tents
  • Traditional Bedouin tea, lunch, dinner, and breakfast included
